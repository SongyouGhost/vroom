禮數搞臺南，臺南搞禮數

C07 臺南光柵印花設計

臺南擁有四百年的建城歷史，同時也經歷過不同國家治理過的城市，具有相當豐富的人文歷史文化，也蘊涵著極具特色的地理風情和物產美食。許多遊客只要來到臺南，就會將臺南所蘊涵的人文歷史、地理風情和物產美食，打包成極具城市魅力的_禮物_持續地往外傳送出去，讓世人知道在這福爾摩沙的小島上，還有一個如此極具特色的城市。
      將臺南所具有的地方特色轉化為”圖像化”的圖騰印花設計，創造屬於臺南每個地方的專屬特色印象圖騰，將每個地方的特點經印象圖騰傳達出去，有利於地方的行銷，協助地方發展。而我們將這些具有地方特色的印花圖騰以一個_禮物盒_的形式，將這份獨特魅力的禮物送給所有喜愛臺南的人文地理和物產美食的收禮者。
       我們將印花圖騰採用移動改變視角的設計，呈現出動態的印花效果，將臺南的動感傳遞到每個人的手中。當人們看到這些動起來的印花時，宛如走動在臺南的街道上，探索著臺南的美景一般，並且發掘出令人難忘的驚喜與豐富的人文特色。

Tainan boasts a 400-year history of city-building and has been governed by various countries, enriching its cultural heritage. The city is imbued with distinctive geographical charm, local specialties, and a rich culinary scene. Many visitors, upon arriving in Tainan, encapsulate its cultural history, geographical allure, and culinary delights into a captivating gift that they continue to share with the world. This helps showcase the unique charm of this city on the island of Formosa to a global audience.

By transforming Tainan's local characteristics into "visualized" totem prints, each area's exclusive impressions can be conveyed through these designs. This approach aids in local marketing and contributes to the development of the region. We present these region-specific printed totems in the form of a gift box, offering this distinctive charm to all aficionados of Tainan's cultural, geographical, and culinary delights.

The printed totem designs feature a perspective-changing pattern, creating a dynamic visual effect that brings Tainan's vibrancy to everyone's hands. As people observe these animated prints, they feel as if they are strolling through Tainan's streets, exploring its scenic beauty, and uncovering unforgettable surprises and rich cultural nuances.

Tainan's essence, encapsulated in these unique gifts, serves as a delightful memento for those who appreciate the city's blend of cultural heritage, geographical allure, and culinary excellence.
