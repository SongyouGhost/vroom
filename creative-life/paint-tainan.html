打印臺南

C07 臺南光柵印花設計

突破既有的直線光柵,「嶄新的放射線光柵設計增添新的效果與趣味。我們以臺南各地特色的人文地理風情美食為概念,製作一系列代表臺南的印花設計,屬於臺南的圖騰,並透過色彩搭配呈現臺南的豐富多元性。

這些印花設計可以應用於不同的紡織品或商品上,讓人們更深入了解臺南的特色文化。同時,透過將印花設計應用於不同的商品上,希望也能夠推動臺南地方產業的發展,讓更多人了解及支持這座城市的發展。

Breaking through the traditional linear grid, the new radiation grid design adds new effects and interests. Taking inspiration from the cultural and geographical beauty of various places in Tainan, we have created a series of prints representing Tainan, serving as totems of the city. Through color combinations, we showcase the rich diversity of Tainan. These print designs can be applied to different textiles or products, allowing people to gain a deeper understanding of Tainan's unique culture. Additionally, by applying these print designs to various products, we hope to promote the development of local industries in Tainan and encourage more people to understand and support the city's growth.
