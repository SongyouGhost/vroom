妖鬼夜市

C12 傳統妖怪與夜市零食包裝

在台灣，有著五個妖怪傳說，它們面臨著與人類共存的生存考驗。這些傳說迫使它們改變形象，利用人類貪吃的本性來融入其中。透過這個過程，它們不斷地突破傳統印象，破除人們對它們的恐懼。這些妖怪與零食結合，誕生出全新的樣貌，進行著一場歷史的再生。

In Taiwan, there are five legendary tales of monsters, facing the challenge of coexistence with humans. These legends compel them to change their images, leveraging human greed to integrate themselves. Through this process, they continuously break through traditional impressions, dispelling people's fears of them. By combining with snacks, these monsters give birth to new appearances, undergoing a rebirth in history.
