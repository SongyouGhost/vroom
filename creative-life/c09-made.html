C09 二手回收再製

「快時尚」的影響灣致衣物汰換的速度變快變多，衍生出許多的衣物垃圾，這些衣物若沒有經過正確回收，將會對環境帶來嚴重影響。同時發現了今年臺灣的在地選舉文化，在選舉期間產生大量的政治廣告宣傳材料，如旗幟、海報、傳單、手冊等，而在選舉結束後卻成為垃圾，也造成資源上的浪費。

為了提升舊衣物使用效益,透過重新設計和再製,將這些衣物可以變得新穎而有價值,延長了它們的使用壽命。因此我們想以「二手回收衣物、選舉布料宣傳品」再製成包,減少丟棄的資源浪費及不必要的消費。

包型上應用東方文化漢服的交領衣襟及衣袖延伸成包包上的領口及包型設計，將漢服的元素運用在包包的設計上，也展現出舊與新結合的概念。

The rapid turnover of clothing due to the influence of "fast fashion" has led to a significant increase in clothing waste, posing serious environmental concerns if not properly recycled. Additionally, the prevalent local election culture in Taiwan generates a substantial amount of political advertising materials, such as banners, posters, leaflets, and brochures, which often become waste after the elections, resulting in resource wastage.To enhance the utility of old clothing, we propose re-designing and repurposing them to give them new value and extend their lifespan. Therefore, we aim to repurpose "second-hand recycled clothing and election campaign materials" into bags, reducing the disposal of resources and unnecessary consumption.
In the design of these bags, we incorporate elements of traditional Chinese clothing, such as the overlapping collar and extended sleeves, to form the neckline and shape of the bags, thus merging the concept of old and new.
