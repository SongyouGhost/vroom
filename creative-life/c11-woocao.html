WOO草~

C11 WOO草IP設計

因為人們不知道，所以統稱它們「雜草」；設定小草的個性，將小草形象化，讓更多人認識台灣小草。隨著社會環境的變化，雜草的地位也大不如前，但其實每個草都有屬於自己的樣貌、特色與功效。Woo草希望讓小草也有機會表現自己的特色與個性，翻轉人們對草的刻板印象，探索日常中常見的小草世界！看見雜草的特別之處，領WOO~ 雜草的世界!

Because people are unaware, they collectively refer to them as "weeds"; setting the personalities of the grasses, characterizing the grasses, and allowing more people to understand Taiwan's grass.

With the changes in the social environment, the status of weeds is not as good as before, but in fact, each grass has its own appearance, characteristics, and effects. Woo Grass hopes to give grasses the opportunity to show their characteristics and personalities, overturning people's stereotypes of grass, and exploring the common world of grass in daily life!

See the uniqueness of weeds, lead WOO~ the world of weeds!
