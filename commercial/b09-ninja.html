B09 家庭溝通與互動設計

有天弟弟與往常一樣在房間裡練習跳舞，沉浸在音樂的世界裡，突然 ！爸爸奪門而入！被嚇爆的弟弟氣得直接衝到爸爸面前把門奪回來說：「要敲門！給我一點個人隱私！」，隨後爸爸敷衍性的敲了兩下門然後說：「你已經１３歲了，再過不久就是你和你姐１４歲的生日，也就代表你是時候該好好學習如何當個稱職的家庭忍者了。」但弟弟並不想要做這些，對他而言，音樂才是讓他真正感受到快樂的東西，但他並沒有與爸爸說這些，而是選擇直接拒絕了爸爸的要求，因此，爸爸決定設下一連串的考驗病根弟弟下了賭注，如果你完成考驗就不用接手家業，但如果你輸了，就要在成年禮時接下這個責任，弟弟無奈只能答應，而他們都沒發現一旁的媽媽及姊姊都在偷聽這一切。

Dad's Test:
One day, as usual, little brother was practicing dancing in his room, immersed in the world of music, when suddenly! Dad barged in! Startled, the little brother stormed up to Dad and snatched the door back, saying, "You need to knock! Give me some privacy!" Dad then half-heartedly knocked twice on the door and said, "You're already 13 years old. Soon, it will be your and your sister's 14th birthday, which means it's time for you to learn how to be a responsible family ninja."But the little brother didn't want any of that. To him, music was the only thing that truly brought him joy. However, he didn't express this to Dad. Instead, he chose to directly refuse Dad's request. Consequently, Dad decided to set up a series of tests and challenged the little brother. If he passed the tests, he wouldn't have to take over the family business. But if he lost, he would have to take on that responsibility at his coming-of-age ceremony. Reluctantly, the little brother agreed, unaware that Mom and his sister were eavesdropping nearby.
