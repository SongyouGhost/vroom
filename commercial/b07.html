Bi！Bi!營養糾察隊

B07 倡導營養均衡教具之研究

藉由「B!B! 營養糾察隊」激發兒童對於飲食均衡和正確攝取營養的的興趣與注意，透過遊戲體驗與故事情節，使大家能了解並感受到追求健康的優點與適量攝取的重要。

"B!B! Nutrition Patrol" is designed to pique children's curiosity and emphasize the importance of consuming a healthy, balanced diet and obtaining the appropriate nutrients. Everyone can understand and experience the benefits of prioritizing health and consuming appropriate amounts through gaming experiences and narratives. Significance.
