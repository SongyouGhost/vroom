Lock for Luck
夯枷解厄

B05 民俗信仰

身戴枷鎖贖罪，解厄消業障。這是臺灣傳統文化的一部分。我們計劃將這些傳統文化轉化為現代思維，並以體驗展覽的方式呈現。這樣，不論是從未聽過這項活動的人，還是曾聽過但未參與過的人，都能透過我們的設計更深入了解！

Wearing shackles to atone for sins, dispelling misfortune and eliminating obstacles. This is a part of Taiwan's traditional culture. We plan to transform these traditional customs into forms understandable by modern people and present them through experiential exhibitions. This way, whether one has never heard of this activity or has heard of it but never participated, they can gain a deeper understanding through our designs.
