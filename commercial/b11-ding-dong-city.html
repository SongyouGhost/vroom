B11 外送主題桌游

探討外送員的工作狀況及各種辛酸事外送產業近幾年因疫情及消費型態的轉變，迅速地進入大眾的生活，而在過於迅速的發展背後卻引起許多的社會問題,造成關於外送的爭議頻繁的出現在新聞上。
我們想透過體驗各式設計產出並模擬「外送城市」的情境，讓他們能夠迅速地共感外送員現在所面臨的社會情況。玩家們身為剛接觸外送產業的菜鳥，在工作途中會遇到各種不同的事件，要在工作中躲過不同困難的危機。
而此時隨著外送產業高速的發展,各種不同的社會新聞也影響了你的工作，如新聞公布平台減薪或是因防疫政策導致訂單需求增加…等，都會影響玩家的整場遊戲中的工作狀況。

Exploring the working conditions and various challenges faced by delivery workers, the delivery industry has rapidly entered the public's life in recent years due to changes in consumer behavior and the COVID-19 pandemic. However, behind this rapid development lie many social issues, leading to frequent controversies about delivery services in the news.
We aim to create various design outputs and simulate the scenario of a "delivery city" to allow participants to quickly empathize with the social situation faced by delivery workers. Players, as newcomers to the delivery industry, will encounter different events during their work and have to navigate through various crises.
As the delivery industry rapidly evolves, different social news events will affect your work, such as platform announcements of wage cuts or increased demand due to epidemic prevention policies. All these factors will influence the players' working conditions throughout the game.
