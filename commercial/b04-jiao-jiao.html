角角 Jiao Jiao

B04 台南官田菱角作物推廣

官田在地農作——菱角，來自台南市官田區的在地農作。除了食用以外，菱角是具有豐富利用價值的季節性作物。我們將菱角仁改良成不用為剝殼所苦、也無須為產季問題煩惱的小零嘴，無論在何時何地，都能夠方便且快速食用美味的菱角即食零嘴——角角。

Guantian Local Farming—Water Chestnuts

Water chestnuts, originating from Guantian District in Tainan City, are local produce. Besides being consumed as food, water chestnuts are seasonal crops with rich utilitarian value.
‍
We have improved water chestnut kernels into a convenient and quick snack called "角角" that does not require the hassle of shelling and is not affected by seasonal availability. Whether it's anytime, anywhere, you can easily and quickly enjoy delicious water chestnut snacks—"Jiao Jiao".
