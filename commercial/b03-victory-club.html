VICTORY MONSTER 福祿獸

B03 招財納福品牌規劃

VICTORY MONSTER以人生五大要素「財富」、「感情」、「健康」、「人際」、「事業」為品牌中心，我們希望向大眾傳達「人生勝利」不只是建立於財富，顧及方方面面，縱觀全局才能創造理想中的人生。

我們建立了「VICTORY MONSTER」，邀請大家加入我們，一起朝著人生勝利組的目標前進。

Centering on the five essential elements of life:"wealth","relationships","health","interpersonal connections, and career", this communicates to the public that being part of the "Life's Winners" is not solely based on wealth.Instead, it involves consideration of all aspects, and only by taking a holistic view can one create the ideal life.

INVITE EVERYONE TO JOIN US VICTORY MONSTER.
