慢慢 MONMON

B02 拖延心理共感設計

慢慢MONMON旨在深入探索拖延行為的多重層面，以梗圖為主軸，透過詼諧的梗圖和文字細分各種拖延行為的類型，幫助參與者更深入地了解自己的拖延特點和行為模式。同時，我們以幽默的方式呈現拖延行為，引導參與者接納自己的拖延特點，並找到適當的方式應對。

透過正向心理學元素的運用，強調拖延不一定是負面行為，有時候也是一種舒壓和自我調適的方式。我們鼓勵參與者通過社群平台分享自己的梗圖創作，促進共享和討論，讓更多人能夠享受到這種輕鬆幽默的拖延觀念。最終，期望透過參與專案，參與者能夠更好地理解自己的拖延行為，並尋找有效的方法來面對，從而實現自我提升和生活品質的提高。

The "MONMON" project aims to delve into the multiple facets of procrastination behavior, with memes as the main focus. Through humorous memes and text, different types of procrastination behaviors are subdivided, helping participants to gain a deeper understanding of their procrastination tendencies and behavior patterns. Additionally, we present procrastination behavior in a humorous way, guiding participants to accept their procrastination tendencies and find appropriate ways to deal with them.

By incorporating elements of positive psychology, we emphasize that procrastination is not necessarily a negative behavior but sometimes a way of relieving stress and self-adjustment. We encourage participants to share their meme creations through social media platforms to promote sharing and discussion, allowing more people to enjoy this relaxed and humorous procrastination concept. Ultimately, through participation in the project, we hope that participants can better understand their procrastination behavior and find effective ways to cope with it, thereby achieving self-improvement and enhancing their quality of life.
