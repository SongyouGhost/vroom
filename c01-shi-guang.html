石光

C01 保護化石專案-沐浴石光泡澡球

將泡澡球的造型外觀結合六種動物型態，內部隱藏著絕種動物化石，倡導環境保育的重要性。選用泡澡球為主體意旨化石就像泡沫般逐漸消失，傳達著化石不斷被肆意帶走的現象發生，也意味著臺南在地的化石出土地「菜寮溪」，每當遇到豪雨沖刷後，就會有化石逐漸出土的過程。自己就如同化石般在菜寮溪中需要被保護，是個重要的存在。沐浴「時」光改為沐浴「石」光，表達著化石在我們的沐浴時光中的存在。

The design of the bath bomb incorporates six animal shapes, with extinct animal fossils hidden inside, advocating for the importance of environmental conservation. Using the bath bomb as the main element symbolizes fossils gradually disappearing like foam, highlighting the ongoing issue of fossil depletion. Additionally, it signifies the fossil excavation site "Cailiao Creek" in Tainan, where fossils gradually surface after heavy rainfall. These fossils, resembling our own existence, need protection as they are significant entities. We change bathing "time" to bathing "stone" to emphasize the importance of fossils in our bathing experience.
