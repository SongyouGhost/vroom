A09 2D動畫

兔女孩帶著恐懼的心情獨自前往教室，進入教室的一瞬間，教室變成了洞穴，兔女孩緊張地四處張望，而在兔女孩身後，有個不明黑影正跟蹤她，兔女孩轉身一看，黑影出現在兔女孩的視角，兔女孩嚇到趕緊逃走，而黑影正在追著她，兔女孩是否找到出口的光芒呢？

The bunny girl, with a fearful heart, went to the classroom alone. The moment she entered, the classroom turned into a cave. The bunny girl nervously looked around, and behind her, an unknown dark shadow was following her. When the bunny girl turned around, the shadow appeared in her view. Frightened, she quickly ran away, with the shadow chasing after her. Will the bunny girl find the light at the end?
