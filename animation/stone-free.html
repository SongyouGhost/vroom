A08 3D動畫

-故事主要講述主角的穿越時空冒險-

主角的父親因實驗意外而失蹤，讓主角決定穿越時空去阻止這場事故發生
她與過去的父親和自己相遇，並試圖翻轉未來。

The story mainly tells the tale of the protagonist's adventure through time and space.

The protagonist's father disappeared due to an experimental accident, prompting her to decide to travel through time and space to prevent the accident.

She encounters her past father and herself and tries to turn the future around.
