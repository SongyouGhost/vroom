A04 動畫製作

主角是不受待見的魔術師，在自己所喜愛的魔術表演上，得不到觀眾的青睞，而另一位魔術師卻能獲得掌聲。而後來主角因為進監獄，得到了他夢寐以求的青睞，他對此感到滿足。但在獄中回想起自己不受歡迎的日子，於是決定透過逃獄，再次滿足心中所渴望的關注。

The protagonist is an unpopular magician who fails to win the audience's favor in his beloved magic performances, while another magician receives applause. Later, the protagonist ends up in prison and finally receives the attention he craves, feeling satisfied with it. However, while reminiscing about his unpopular days in prison, he decides to escape and once again satisfy his desire for attention.
