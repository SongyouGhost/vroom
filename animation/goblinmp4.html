Goblin.mp4

A07 2D 動畫製作

如果一個動畫角色獲得了意識，他會做出什麼選擇？想要自由、渴望力量變成真實的存在…還是乖乖接受自己的命運？

故事發生在一座金字塔裡，哥布林在遺跡裡挖寶時，發生了怪事。他得到了不知從何而來的魔法鏡頭，被迫與巨蜥展開一場決鬥。哥布林在魔法鏡頭的幫助下取得了不可能的勝利。但影片卻到此為止了…？

If an animated character gained consciousness, what choices would they make? Would they desire freedom, crave power to become a real existence... or obediently accept their fate?

The story takes place in a pyramid, where a goblin encounters strange events while treasure hunting in ruins. He obtains a magical camera from an unknown source and is forced into a duel with a giant lizard. With the help of the magical camera, the goblin achieves an impossible victory. But the video ends there...?
